Data integration and multi-omics methods are emerging as valuable tools for understanding the functioning of complex biological systems. Data integration is defined as a process by which data from different sources are combined statistically to make large-scale conclusions about the disease and to obtain a comprehensive view of biological processes. Multi-omics approaches integrate data from different omics levels to understand their combined influence on the biological process. While there is a plethora of multi-omics data produced and collected, their integration remains challenging from both technical and ontological perspectives. Join us at MODI, where experts in multi-omics and data integration come together to explore cutting-edge methodologies for understanding complex biological systems.
